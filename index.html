Quick Answers

Questions We Hear Every Day

Most residential siding projects take between 2 and 5 days from start to finish, depending on the size of the home and the scope of work. A typical 2,000 sq ft colonial replacement takes 3 days with our full crew. Larger homes or projects involving significant trim work, soffit and fascia replacement, or complex architectural details may take 5–7 days. We'll provide a specific timeline with your estimate so you know exactly what to expect.
James Hardie fiber cement siding is a composite material made from cement, sand, and cellulose fibers — engineered to resist moisture, impact, insects, and fire. Unlike wood, it doesn't rot or warp. Unlike vinyl, it doesn't melt or fade in extreme heat. It's the most durable siding option on the market and comes with a 30-year non-prorated warranty. As a James Hardie Preferred Contractor, we're certified to install HardiePlank, HardieShingle, HardieTrim, and the full ColorPlus technology pre-painted line.
Both are premium siding products, but they're made from different materials. James Hardie is a fiber cement product — heavier, extremely hard, and non-combustible. LP SmartSide is an engineered wood product — lighter, easier to install, and designed to replicate the look of natural wood with a 50-year warranty. For Chicago's climate, both perform exceptionally well when installed correctly. James Hardie is often preferred for maximum durability; LP SmartSide is preferred when homeowners want a natural wood aesthetic. We'll walk through both options during your estimate.

If the claim is approved, your insurance typically covers the cost of material and installation minus your deductible. We've helped hundreds of DuPage County homeowners navigate the insurance process and can tell you within the first visit whether your damage is likely claim-eligible.

The selection depends on the product. James Hardie ColorPlus Technology offers over 30 pre-painted colors baked on at the factory with a 15-year fade warranty. LP SmartSide comes primed for painting in any exterior color. Vinyl siding is available in dozens of manufacturer colors that are built into the material — so there's no paint to chip, peel, or fade. We keep sample boards for all major lines so you can see colors in natural light at your home before making a decision.
Properly installed fiber cement siding lasts 40–50 years with minimal maintenance — periodic painting every 10–15 years on non-ColorPlus products. LP SmartSide is rated for 50 years. Vinyl siding has a 20–40 year lifespan depending on quality and climate exposure. In Chicago's climate, material choice, installation technique, and fastening method all significantly affect longevity. We've been doing this since 2008 and our earliest installs are still holding up beautifully.
Our workmanship warranty covers the quality and integrity of our installation for the life of the project on qualifying work. This includes proper fastening, weatherproofing, flashing, trim integration, and any issues that arise from our installation process. It's separate from and in addition to the manufacturer's material warranty. If anything related to our installation is ever not right, we come back and fix it at no charge — that's been our policy since day one.
