Siding Contractor in Plainfield, IL

Plainfield has seen explosive growth over the past two decades, becoming one of the fastest-growing communities in Will County. That growth has created enormous demand for quality siding installation — both on new construction and on homes built during the boom years that are now ready for their first major exterior refresh.

Plainfield: Where Builder Vinyl Meets Its Replacement Window

Plainfield's growth trajectory has produced a housing market with a specific challenge: a large cohort of homes built between 1995 and 2010 that originally came with builder-grade vinyl siding. That siding is now 15 to 30 years old and reaching the end of its useful life. Homeowners in neighborhoods like Springbank, Whispering Creek, and Grande Park are replacing original builder siding at a high rate, and the upgrade choices they make now will determine their home's exterior performance for the next three decades.

The most common upgrade we see in Plainfield is from original builder vinyl to James Hardie fiber cement. The improvement in durability, dimensional stability, and fire resistance is significant — and the curb appeal difference is immediately noticeable. James Hardie's ColorPlus factory-finished options allow homeowners to choose from over 30 colors with a 15-year finish warranty, eliminating the maintenance cycle of regular repainting.

New construction continues at a rapid pace in Plainfield, particularly in the developments west of Route 59. Ridgeline's commercial division works with several active builders in the area, providing siding installation on new homes as part of general contractor relationships. If you're building a new home in Plainfield and want to specify James Hardie or LP SmartSide rather than builder-grade vinyl, we can provide pricing that works within a new construction budget.

Will County's climate doesn't differ significantly from DuPage County to the north, but Plainfield's location in the western reaches of the metro does expose it to slightly higher wind events. We factor local wind load requirements into our installation specs. We serve all Plainfield zip codes including 60544 and 60585.
