Siding Contractor in Aurora, IL

Aurora is Illinois's second-largest city and one of the most active housing markets in the Chicago metro. Ridgeline Exteriors has been serving Aurora homeowners with professional siding installation and repair since 2008 — from the historic neighborhoods near downtown to the newer developments on the city's eastern edge.

Siding in Aurora: Scale and Quality

Aurora spans two counties — DuPage and Kane — and encompasses an enormous variety of housing stock. From Victorian-era homes near the Fox River to large suburban colonials in the Oakhurst and Hometown neighborhoods, the city's exterior siding needs are as diverse as its architectural history. Ridgeline Exteriors brings the same certified installation standards to every corner of Aurora regardless of home size, age, or style.

The Fox River corridor creates specific challenges for exterior materials. Homes near the river experience higher humidity and occasional flooding that can accelerate moisture damage in wood and traditional materials. James Hardie fiber cement siding is particularly well-suited to Aurora's riverfront properties — its concrete composite construction is impervious to moisture infiltration, and it carries a 30-year non-prorated warranty that doesn't exclude moisture-related issues when properly installed.

Aurora also experiences some of the strongest wind events in the Chicago metro during spring storm season. We've repaired hundreds of Aurora homes following hail and wind damage, and we work directly with insurance adjusters to help homeowners navigate storm damage claims. If your Aurora home has sustained siding damage, we can typically provide a damage assessment report within 48 hours.

For Aurora's newer construction neighborhoods, LP SmartSide engineered wood has become increasingly popular — it delivers the natural wood aesthetic that many newer homes are designed around without the maintenance demands of real cedar or pine. The product's 50-year warranty makes it a compelling long-term investment for Aurora homeowners planning to stay in their homes.

We serve all of Aurora's zip codes including 60502, 60503, 60504, 60505, 60506, and 60507. Whether you need a full replacement, storm damage repair, or just soffit and fascia work along your roofline, our licensed Aurora crews are ready to help. Free estimates are available by appointment and we respond within two business hours.
