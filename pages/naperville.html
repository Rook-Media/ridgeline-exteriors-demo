Siding Contractor in Naperville, IL

Ridgeline Exteriors was founded right here in Naperville. For over 17 years, we've been the community's James Hardie Preferred siding contractor — installing fiber cement, vinyl, and engineered wood siding on homes from the Highlands to River Run and every neighborhood in between.

Why Naperville Homeowners Choose Fiber Cement

Naperville sits squarely in the heart of DuPage County, where winters drop well below zero and summers push into the 90s. That 110-degree seasonal temperature swing is one of the harshest environments for exterior building materials in the country. Wood siding cracks, swells, and rots. Vinyl can warp or become brittle in extreme cold. Fiber cement, particularly James Hardie's HardiePlank, was engineered specifically for freeze-thaw climates like ours.

As a James Hardie Preferred Contractor — a designation earned through documented installation history, technical training, and manufacturer review — Ridgeline Exteriors is one of only a small number of contractors in DuPage County authorized to install the full Hardie product line, including HardiePlank lap siding, HardieShingle, HardieTrim, and the factory-painted ColorPlus Technology collection. ColorPlus products carry a 15-year warranty on the finish itself, meaning no repainting for the better part of two decades.

Naperville's housing stock is diverse — you'll find everything from 1960s ranch homes near downtown to new construction in Springbrook Prairie and large Colonials in Cress Creek. We've installed on all of them. Our crews understand local HOA color guidelines, DuPage County permit requirements, and the specific challenges of installing around Naperville's mature tree canopy, which can complicate access and require careful staging.

Beyond fiber cement, we install LP SmartSide engineered wood siding — a premium alternative for homeowners who want the warmth and texture of real wood without the maintenance burden. LP SmartSide is rated for 50 years and holds up beautifully in Naperville's climate when installed correctly with proper flashing and fastening techniques. We also offer insulated vinyl siding from leading manufacturers, which is particularly popular with homeowners looking to improve energy efficiency alongside curb appeal.

Every Ridgeline project starts with a free, no-obligation estimate at your home. We measure your home, assess the existing siding for any rot, moisture damage, or structural issues that need addressing before installation, and walk you through the full range of options with physical sample boards so you can see colors in your home's actual light conditions. There are no hidden fees and no pressure sales tactics — just honest recommendations from a contractor who plans to be in this community for another 17 years.

Our service area spans all of Naperville's zip codes — 60540, 60563, 60564, and 60565 — as well as neighboring communities in Aurora, Lisle, Woodridge, Bolingbrook, and Plainfield. We're local, we're licensed, and we're insured. Call us or submit a request below and we'll be in touch within two business hours.
