Builder Partnerships

On Time, On Spec, Every Time

Builders and general contractors need siding subcontractors who show up when scheduled, install to specification, and do not create downstream problems for finishers, painters, or building inspectors. Late or incorrect siding installation can cascade into significant schedule delays on a construction project — delaying certificate of occupancy, disrupting closing timelines, and creating cost overruns that damage builder-buyer relationships.

Ridgeline Exteriors has built a reputation among western suburban builders for exactly the reliability that construction schedules demand. We maintain enough crew capacity to honor our start commitments even during peak season, we review construction documents and manufacturer specifications before mobilizing, and we flag any conflicts between the plans and actual field conditions before they become problems. Our project managers communicate directly with construction superintendents throughout the installation and we never leave a site without documenting our work-in-place for the next trade.

For James Hardie installations, we follow the Hardie Installation Specification to the letter — proper fastening patterns, required clearances from grade and roofing, correct end-joint treatment, and flashing details that pass building department inspection on the first attempt. This matters on new construction because Hardie's full warranty is only available when installation meets their published specifications, and warranty issues that surface after closing create expensive and reputation-damaging callbacks for builders.

New Construction Services

What We Deliver for Builders

Custom Home Siding

Architectural siding profiles for custom and semi-custom homes. We work with architects and designers to execute complex elevations including mixed cladding, board-and-batten accents, shingle-style dormers, and full HardieWrap weather barrier systems. Our crews are experienced with the details that make custom homes distinctive and that homeowners notice and remember.

Production Home Communities

High-volume subdivision siding for production builders with multiple lots closing simultaneously. We maintain dedicated crews for production accounts and prioritize schedule commitments for communities where closing delays have direct financial consequences for our builder partners.

Multi-Family New Construction

New apartment and condo developments where GCs need a siding subcontractor with commercial insurance, proper bonding, and the crew capacity to work on multiple buildings simultaneously. We provide material submittals, RFI response, and change order documentation consistent with commercial GC requirements.

Commercial Ground-Up

New commercial construction including retail centers, office buildings, light industrial, and mixed-use developments. We coordinate with structural steel, EIFS, and masonry trades to sequence the siding scope correctly and we provide the lien waivers and insurance certificates that commercial GCs require on every pay application.
