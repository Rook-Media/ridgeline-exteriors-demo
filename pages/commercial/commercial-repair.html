Commercial Siding Repair

From storm damage response to localized deterioration, Ridgeline Exteriors handles commercial siding repair with the documentation, color matching, and crew availability that property managers demand.

Commercial Repair Specialists

Minimizing Disruption, Maximizing Documentation

When a storm damages a commercial or multi-family property, property managers face pressure from multiple directions simultaneously: tenants demanding resolution, insurance adjusters requesting documentation, ownership asking for cost estimates, and the risk of ongoing moisture intrusion if the envelope is compromised. Ridgeline Exteriors responds quickly, documents thoroughly, and executes repairs with the quality that prevents repeat calls.

Our commercial repair team is available for emergency response following significant weather events — hailstorms, straight-line wind events, and derecho damage are recurring realities for western suburban properties. We conduct damage assessments within 24 to 48 hours of a significant storm event, provide photo-documented scope reports suitable for insurance submission, and can implement emergency tarping or temporary boarding where moisture exposure is immediate.

For non-emergency commercial repair — deteriorating siding sections, impact damage, failed caulking at penetrations, or rotted substrate behind existing cladding — we provide written scopes with detailed pricing and work within property maintenance schedules to minimize disruption to tenants, parking, and common areas. We maintain inventory of the most common commercial siding profiles used in our service area to accelerate material availability and reduce lead times on repairs.

Repair Services

Commercial Repair Capabilities

Storm Damage Assessment

Post-storm inspection with photo documentation, measurement of affected areas, and scope reports formatted for insurance carrier submission. We work with all major commercial carriers and can communicate directly with adjusters on your behalf.

Hail Damage Repair

Hail damage on vinyl siding often affects all elevations uniformly. We assess whether repair or full replacement is the appropriate response based on impact density and document both options for insurance claim decision-making.

Wind Damage Repair

High-wind events frequently dislodge siding panels, tear soffit sections, and damage fascia. We provide rapid response for wind damage to restore the building envelope, re-secure loose material, and address any resulting moisture exposure.

Localized Deterioration

Section-by-section replacement of failed or deteriorating siding on buildings where full replacement is not yet warranted. We color-match to existing material and document the work for reserve fund records.

Substrate Repair

Rotted OSB sheathing, failed house wrap, and deteriorated framing members discovered behind existing siding. We repair or replace substrate before re-siding to ensure the new installation has a sound base and to prevent recurring moisture problems.

Caulking & Sealant

Failed or missing sealant at windows, doors, penetrations, and trim terminations is one of the most common sources of moisture intrusion in commercial buildings. We provide comprehensive re-caulking scopes for properties where sealant maintenance has been deferred.
