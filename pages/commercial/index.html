Why Choose Us

What Sets Our Commercial Division Apart

Dedicated Project Management

Every commercial project has a named project manager who is your single point of contact from bid to closeout. No routing through a general call center.

Schedule Reliability

We maintain crew capacity to honor our start commitments. We do not overbook and we do not push commercial projects to accommodate residential overflow.

Commercial-Grade Documentation

Material submittals, lien waivers, permit records, insurance certificates, and warranty registrations — all provided as a matter of course, not as an extra.

Crew Capacity

We run multiple commercial crews simultaneously, which allows us to staff multi-building projects properly and work building-by-building rather than piecemeal.

Occupied Building Protocols

Tenant notification templates, daily site cleanup standards, access window restrictions, and respectful job site behavior in occupied residential environments.

James Hardie Preferred

As a Hardie Preferred Contractor since 2013, we are authorized to offer the full 30-year product and 15-year ColorPlus finish warranties on all commercial Hardie installations.

Our Process

How a Commercial Project Unfolds

01

Initial Inquiry

Call, email, or submit our contact form. Our commercial team responds within one business day and schedules an initial site walk within 5 days.

02

Site Assessment

We walk the full property, document existing conditions, and identify substrate issues, scope variables, and material options. Assessment is at no cost.

03

Written Proposal

Detailed line-item proposal delivered within 48 hours of the site walk. Includes scope, phasing options, material specs, warranty details, and pricing.

04

Board / Stakeholder Approval

For HOA and ownership-group projects, we attend meetings, answer questions, and provide supporting materials for the approval process.

05

Installation

Phased, documented installation with daily progress updates, proactive communication on any field changes, and strict site cleanliness standards throughout.

06

Closeout

Final walkthrough, permit closure, warranty registration, and delivery of complete project documentation binder. Relationship doesn't end at the final invoice.
