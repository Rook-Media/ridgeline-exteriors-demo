Multi-Family Expertise

Proven Protocols for Occupied Properties

Multi-family siding work is a discipline of its own. You are not just replacing siding — you are managing the experience of dozens or hundreds of residents who are living in the buildings you are working on simultaneously. That means early morning restrictions, controlled debris management, clear communication with tenants, and crews who understand that a resident's quality of life is directly affected by how professionally the job is run.

Ridgeline Exteriors has developed and refined protocols specifically for occupied multi-family work over a decade of apartment complex and condo community projects. We pre-notify tenants of the work schedule, provide a dedicated on-site project manager for every multi-family job, and sequence building-by-building or section-by-section to minimize the footprint of active construction at any given time. We do not begin work before 7:30 a.m. on occupied residential properties and we require daily site cleanup before leaving each evening.

Phased budgeting is also a reality for multi-family associations and property management companies. Many of our long-term multi-family clients have worked with us across multi-year contracts that address a community building by building, aligned with annual budget cycles. We honor the pricing and scope terms established at the outset of a multi-phase contract and we provide reserve fund documentation to support your planning.

Property Types We Serve

Multi-Family Projects We Handle

Apartment Complexes

Garden-style and mid-rise apartment communities with 20 to 300+ units. We coordinate with property management on tenant notification, parking logistics, and phased building sequencing. Emergency ingress/egress is maintained at all times.

Condominium Communities

Condo associations with common exterior responsibility. We work directly with boards and their property managers, provide formal bid documentation suitable for board meetings, and can present to ownership groups when needed.

Townhome Communities

Attached and detached townhome developments with HOA-managed exteriors. We can work on a community-wide contract basis or address individual buildings. Color and material consistency across units is a specialty.

Senior Living Facilities

Independent and assisted living communities where resident disruption must be minimized. We adapt our schedules and on-site behavior to the specific sensitivity of senior living environments, coordinating closely with facility administration.

Market-Rate Rental Properties

Investment property owners seeking to upgrade exterior appearance for tenant retention or to reposition a property in the rental market. We provide ROI-conscious material recommendations that deliver curb appeal at realistic price points.

New Multi-Family Construction

Ground-up multi-family projects where we partner with general contractors from framing stage through final exterior envelope. We provide material submittals, coordinate with the GC schedule, and meet rough-schedule milestones required by construction lenders.
